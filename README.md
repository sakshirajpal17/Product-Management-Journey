# Product Management Journey

A portfolio of product management artifacts across two product case studies: **FitTrack Pro** (AI-powered fitness & wellness app) and **EduLearn Hub** (online learning platform). Includes market research, feature brainstorming, prioritization, roadmaps, Gantt charts, and Jira backlogs.
